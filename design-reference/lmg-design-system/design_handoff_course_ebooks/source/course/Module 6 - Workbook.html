Rehearse the Seven Objections

In your own voice

Draft your response to each — in your voice. Then say each one out loud once. You'll never freeze on these again.

1"That's above our budget."
2"Other creators charge way less."
3"We can only do $X maximum."
4"Can you be flexible on price?"
5"We're offering great exposure."
6"Standard rate, take it or leave it."
7Stalled — silence after "I'll get back to you."
Model · "Above our budget"

"I understand — let's look at what we can adjust. We could reduce the deliverables, shorten the usage window, or drop the exclusivity to bring this closer to your budget."

These are the same seven the Negotiation Assistant covers in Step 3 — your answers here become your starting point when you use the tool.

E

Red Flags Watchlist

If you see these, slow down

If any show up, slow down — they may be reasons to walk, not bumps to push through.

⚑Won't discuss payment until after content is made
⚑Unlimited deliverables / usage / exclusivity for minimal pay
⚑Offers "exposure" instead of money
⚑Refuses to put anything in writing (or gets defensive)
Quick win

Get everything in writing before any work begins — deliverables, payment amount & schedule, timeline, usage, exclusivity, revisions. No contract from them? Send a summary email and ask them to confirm. We build the real contract in Module 7.
