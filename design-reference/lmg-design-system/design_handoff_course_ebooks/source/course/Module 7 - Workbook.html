The 8-Section Contract Review

Your reusable checklist

Walk every contract through these eight sections. If you can't check a box, fix it before you sign.

Required · all six
1
Parties & ScopeCorrect legal name (you / your entity); effective date; brand entity + contact named.
2
Deliverables & TimelinePlatform, type, qty, due date, specs, approval days, min days live (≥30).
3
Payment Terms≥50% upfront, Net 30 or shorter, method specified, late fee (~1.5%/mo).
4
Usage Rights & LicensingDuration limited (1yr default), platforms specified, organic vs paid, whitelisting only if paid, no "general marketing."
5
Revisions & ApprovalsMax 2–3 rounds; brand turnaround capped; "revision" vs "new work" distinguished.
8
FTC Compliance & DisclosuresVariation matches deal (Standard / Ambassador / Affiliate / Gifted); language + placement specified.
Optional · only if part of the deal
6
Exclusivity TermsSpecific competitor categories (not "any in the space"); period capped; paid for separately.
7
Kill Fee & Termination · rec. above ~$500Kill fee % (25–50% after work begins, 100% after delivery); termination notice period.

Can't tick a box? That's the negotiation you still need to have — take it back to Module 6 before signing.

FTC Disclosure Cheat Sheet

Print it · save it · never guess

One reference card for every sponsored post. The fines fall on you — so get this right every time.

The four rules

Clear & conspicuousImpossible to miss — never buried after twenty hashtags.
Unambiguous language✓ #ad · #sponsored · #partner
✗ #collab · #sp · "thanks [brand]"
Cover every frameStories need disclosure on every frame, not just the first.
Tags don't replace it"Paid Partnership" tag supplements — never replaces.

Format by format

FormatDisclosure rule
Feed postIn caption, before the "more" button (first 125 characters).
StoriesOn every frame of sponsored content (text sticker is fine).
Reels / TikTokIn caption and verbally/visually in the video (first 30 sec).
Affiliate links"May earn a commission" — even without direct payment.
Gifted productStill required — #gifted or "[brand] sent me this."
