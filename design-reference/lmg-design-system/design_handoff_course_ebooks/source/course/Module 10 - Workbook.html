Red Flags Watchlist

Run every deal past this

One flag may be explainable. Two or three together is a pattern — and patterns don't reverse. Print this, keep it close, run every deal past it before you sign.

The one rule that matters most

Pattern recognition beats any single flag. One thing might be a disorganized brand — workable. Two or three together is a pattern. Trust it — and decline or restructure cleanly.

The Decline-Gracefully Template

Saying no well

Saying no well is what gives you the bandwidth to say yes to the right deals. Don't over-explain — swap the highlighted line for your scenario.

Re: Partnership opportunity
Thanks so much for thinking of me for this — I appreciate you reaching out. After looking at the details, this particular opportunity isn't quite the right fit for my business right now. [insert reason line] I'd love to stay in touch though, and if something different comes up down the line, please don't hesitate to reach out.
