How the money works

The six ways creators get paid

"Brand deal" is a vague word for six very different revenue models — and most creators only ever use one.

Flat-fee sponsorships, affiliate commission, performance bonuses, usage and licensing, ambassador retainers, and equity or product deals each pay differently and scale differently. The professionals stack them. Knowing all six is how you stop leaving money on the table.

04
Know your leverage

Your pricing-power number

One number does more to justify your rate than your follower count ever will: your engagement rate.

It's the clearest proof that people don't just see your content — they act on it. Calculate it once and you'll negotiate from evidence instead of hope.

ER = (likes + comments + saves) ÷ reach × 100 Above 3% signals a genuinely engaged audience — and real pricing power.
