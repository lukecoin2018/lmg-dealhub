Marker study

Three options on the light treatment. The pin tip pinpoints the exact city while the body stays readable when cities cluster — so the teardrop with a soft glow is the recommendation.

Simple dot

Cleanest, but no anchor point — ambiguous which pixel is the city, and large/small dots read as "more vs less" rather than hub vs satellite.

Teardrop pin

Tip anchors the precise location; the body carries color and size hierarchy. Map-native and instantly legible.

Glowing teardrop Recommended

The teardrop plus a soft brand-color bloom. Adds the premium atmosphere the brief wants and makes hubs feel "lit" without any network lines.

Clustered-Europe legibility

London, Paris, Madrid and Barcelona sit close together. Sized teardrops with a hairline halo stay distinct in both treatments; this is also where a revealed city label would sit.
