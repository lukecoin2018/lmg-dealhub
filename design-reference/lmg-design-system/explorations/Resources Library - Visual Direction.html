Visual Direction · Homepage §4

The Resources Library

Section §4 is not a toolbox of small utilities — it is a newsstand of 23 premium, professionally designed publications: multi-page ebooks, strategy frameworks, reports, and toolkits, each with a magazine-grade cover. The cover artwork is the hero asset, so every direction below leans fully visual and lets the covers run large. Downloads are free, instant, and ungated, so a one-tap Download sits on every item. Five cover-led directions — four from the brief and one invented — each shown in light and dark, with motion and interaction annotated. All motion respects prefers-reduced-motion.

DIRECTION 01

Newsstand

Covers stand face-out on a shelf, like premium magazines on a rack. The artwork dominates; type label and title sit minimally beneath, and the free Download reveals on hover. Two shelves browse horizontally — the rack keeps going, so the depth of the library is felt by scrolling, not counting.

Motion: cover lifts off the shelf on hover Interaction: horizontal scroll-snap rack Build: low — CSS only

DIRECTION 02

Cover Marquee

A continuous river of covers in two counter-scrolling rows — a deep library, always in motion. Hover a row to pause it and grab a cover; the cover lifts and offers its Download. The opposing drift directions make the wall of artwork feel alive without ever demanding a click.

Motion: infinite counter-scroll, edge-masked Interaction: hover-to-pause per row Build: low — CSS keyframes, reduced-motion → scroll
Light
Dark · evening browsing
DIRECTION 03

Fanned Spread

Covers fanned like magazines spread on a table, overlapping and rotated. The deck auto-riffles every few seconds; click any cover to pull it to the front, where its title, type, level, and a prominent Download read out in the panel beside it. Tactile and honest to "these are real documents."

Motion: auto-riffle deck, front card lifts Interaction: click to bring to front Build: med — shown here click+auto; full drag-physics riffle needs a gesture lib

DIRECTION 04

Featured + Library

One lead release runs large — full cover, description, and a prominent Download — paired with a kinetic library of the rest drifting in two vertical columns beside it. Editorial "this month's pick" energy, while the moving columns signal there are many more behind it.

Motion: two vertical marquees, opposite directions Interaction: hover pauses + lifts a cover Build: low — CSS, one featured slot

DIRECTION 05 · INVENTED

The Living Cover Wall

Every one of the 23 covers, packed edge-to-edge into a full-bleed mosaic wall — the entire library visible at a glance is the depth signal. The wall parallax-drifts as you move the mouse; hover any cover and it pops forward, straightens, and the rest dim back, surfacing its Download. Not a row of equal cards — a statement wall of artwork you reach into.

Motion: mouse-parallax drift across depth layers Interaction: hover-to-pop + spotlight dim Build: med — transforms + pointer parallax; reduced-motion → static wall
Light
Dark · evening browsing — the dramatic default

Recommendation

Lead with Direction 04 — Featured + Library for the homepage slot. It does both of §4's jobs at once: the large lead cover proves the production quality, while the two drifting columns make the depth of the library obvious — and it carries one unambiguous, ungated Download as the hero action without burying it in motion. It also degrades gracefully: under reduced motion the columns become a quiet scroll, and the featured release still anchors the section.

Pair it with Direction 05 — The Living Cover Wall on the dedicated /resources page, where a full-bleed, all-23 statement wall has room to breathe and rewards exploration. Newsstand (01) and Cover Marquee (02) are the low-risk, CSS-only fallbacks if engineering wants to ship fast; Fanned Spread (03) is the showpiece to revisit once a drag-gesture budget exists. Across all five: covers stay the star, the audience color-code (blue brands · pink creators · yellow universal) rides the accent line, and the View all 23 resources → route is always one control away.
