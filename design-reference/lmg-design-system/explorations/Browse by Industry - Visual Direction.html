DIRECTION A

Light mode

Default site theme. White ground, photo tiles, warm-neutral door, accents on the counts.

DIRECTION B

Dark mode

Evening-browsing theme. Same tokens, swapped — photos and counts carry over, the door and strip restyle to the dark surface.

Recommendation: ship treatment C (glass plate) across all six tiles. It keeps photography full-bleed so the grid feels like a magazine, while the frosted type-safe plate makes the count bulletproof over whatever imagery lands later. Counts rotate yellow → pink → blue for rhythm (only the “+” and the rule carry accent — the digits stay white for contrast). The door tile is the pressure-release valve, and the platform strip stays a single subordinate row: Instagram and TikTok lead as a balanced pair, the rest are quiet links. One component, one light/dark token swap.
