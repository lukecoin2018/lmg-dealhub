Four pricing rules

1. Never discount below 2× monthly one-off pricing. 2. If exclusivity's included, price it in separately. 3. Build a renewal increase (10–20%) into the initial agreement. 4. Index add-ons to your one-off rates, not the retainer's discounted rate.

The relational basics

Two things that matter more than any tactic: respond to brand emails within 24 hours (even just "got it, will reply properly tomorrow"), and say thank you when they're flexible or generous. The retainers that last are the ones where the human relationship is good.
